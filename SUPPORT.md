# Support

The Nexus Anime project is currently in active development (pre-release, milestones M3–M7). Here is how to get help depending on who you are and what you need.

---

## For users (pre-launch)

The product is not yet publicly available. If you are testing a preview build:

- **Bug reports and feedback:** open a [Bug Report issue](https://github.com/OWNER/nexus-anime/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml). Use the template — it asks for the information we need to act.
- **Ideas and feature requests:** open a [Feature Request issue](https://github.com/OWNER/nexus-anime/issues/new?template=feature_request.yml).
- **General questions:** use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/OWNER/nexus-anime/discussions). This is the right place for "how do I..." or "what about..." conversations that are not concrete bugs or features.

Response time during pre-release is best-effort. We are a small team.

---

## For contributors

- **Setup problems:** open an issue with the `scope:ci` label and the details from the template.
- **Architecture questions:** read the [ADRs](docs/architecture/adr/) first, then open a Discussion if something is unclear.
- **Process questions** (branching, PRs, versioning): the [Repository Convention](docs/REPOSITORY-DESIGN.md) is the source of truth. If it is ambiguous, open a PR against that file.

---

## For security researchers

**Do not use the public issue tracker.** See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for the private reporting channel and our responsible-disclosure commitments.

## Good to know

- The project is **pre-release.** Do not rely on it for anything production-critical.
- The public API surface is **not stable** until `v1.0.0`. Anything may change without notice before then.
- We do not offer paid support tiers at this stage.
